Privacy policy

How inquiry and project information is handled.

This page explains what information EngineeringXYZ collects through the website and how that information is used when someone reaches out about a project.

Effective date: April 29, 2026.

Information we collect

  • Contact details such as name, email address, and phone number.
  • Project details you choose to share through the contact form or email.
  • Technical information needed to operate the site, such as browser and device data.

How information is used

  • To respond to inquiries and discuss potential project work.
  • To understand project requirements and prepare follow-up communication.
  • To maintain and improve website reliability and form delivery.

Sharing and service providers

EngineeringXYZ does not sell personal information. Information may be processed through trusted service providers used for website hosting, email, form delivery, and cloud infrastructure where needed to operate the site and respond to inquiries.

Retention

Inquiry information is kept only as long as needed for communication, project follow-up, recordkeeping, or legal and operational obligations.

Your choices

You can request access, correction, or deletion of the information you submitted by emailing info@engineeringxyz.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to info@engineeringxyz.com.
