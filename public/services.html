Best time to bring us in

When the bottleneck is already visible

We are especially useful when a manual process is too fragile, a fixture family needs cleanup, prototype delays keep compounding, or an automation station needs a better sequence, layout, or transfer strategy.

Project fit

Good for focused scopes and embedded support

Work can be scoped as a targeted machine concept, a specific tooling package, or ongoing support around a live equipment program for hardware startups, small manufacturers, R&D groups, and production teams.

Ways to engage

Different support modes, same technical lane.

Whether the need is a short burst of design work or longer support across a program, the scope stays centered on physical engineering outcomes.

Design sprint
