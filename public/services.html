Services

Engineering support built around production hardware.

The offer centers on machine prototypes, engineering drawings, fixtures, tooling, and production equipment support that helps teams move faster on the floor.

Machine prototypes

Custom prototype stations and production hardware

Concept development, motion studies, machine architecture, detailed design, and drawing release packages for equipment that has to prove itself in real line conditions.

  • Machine concepts and architecture.
  • Part transfer and mechanism design.
  • Assembly drawings and fabrication details.
  • Guarding, access, and operator-flow reviews.
  • Build-focused station refinement and release updates.
Prototype architecture

Sequences that turn bottlenecks into controlled flow

Station layouts and machine sequencing for guarded transfer, timing control, part presentation, and repeatable cycle behavior.

  • Part-entry and discharge strategy.
  • Transfer-state and timing design.
  • Operator interaction and recovery flow.
  • Service access and station reset behavior.
Fixtures and tooling

Workholding, nests, and inspection support

Fixture design for alignment, handling, inspection, poka-yoke, ergonomic interaction, and clear drawing handoff at the station level.

  • Workholding and locating features.
  • Inspection and handling fixtures.
  • Operator-friendly changeover tooling.
  • Detail drawings and fixture release support.
  • Error-proofing and part orientation support.
Inspection and handling

Targeted equipment improvement

Production support that stays grounded in mechanical execution, from gantries and robot cells to staging, routing, and inspection hardware.

  • Part-transfer automation concepts.
  • Production rate improvement studies.
  • Equipment modification and retrofit support.
  • Inspection station and handling integration.
Typical deliverables

What you can expect at handoff.

  • Concept packages and machine-sequence direction.
  • Build-ready machine drawings, fixture drawings, and release packages.
  • Guarding, access, and operator-use reviews.
  • Inspection and handling hardware details.

Best time to bring us in

Project fit
